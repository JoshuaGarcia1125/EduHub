Title: The divider: whole number remainders 
Description: Solve whole number division problems such as 157/6. Use a partitioning tool to help solve randomly generated division problems. Learn strategies to do complex arithmetic in your head. Split a division problem into parts that are easy to work with. This learning object is one in a series of four objects. 
Topics: Area; Arrays; Division; Factors; Number patterns

Key learning objectives:

Educational value:

  • Students recognise factors and multiples.
  • Students recall multiplication and division facts.
  • Random generation of division problems supports repeated use.
  • Times tables can be looked up during learning activities.
