Title: Meet a scientist: environmental scientist 
Description: Explore information about an environmental scientist. Learn about their life and work. Find out why they enjoy their area of research. Choose statements that match their biographical profile. This learning object is one in a series of nine objects. Some objects in the series are also packaged as combined learning objects.

Key learning objectives:
  • Students explore biographical information about everyday scientists, including their professional activities and personal interests.
  • Students explore the diversity of scientific activities.
  • Students compile a biographical summary about an everyday scientist, using information presented in a range of media types.
Educational value:
  • Illustrates how scientists live and work.
  • Provides biographical information about a scientist, their professional activities and personal interests.
  • Includes interview transcripts, sound clips, photos and videos.
  • Demonstrates real-world applications of science.
